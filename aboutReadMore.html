What are sports camps ?

The sports camp provided by ELEVEN STARS SPORT company is a comprehensive institution for sports services, covering individual and team sports in all their aspects. The camp is complete with various elements, including medical examinations, morning and evening training sessions, rehabilitation programs, and improving physical fitness levels, as well as high-level trial matches.

Whom do we offer our sports camp services to?

The sports camps offered by ELEVEN STARS SPORTS Company specifically cater to children and youth who are enrolled in sports schools and sports departments within educational institutions. These sports camps are organized year-round, with a more intensive schedule during summer and winter vacations.
